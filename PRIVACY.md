# Privacy Policy for SAC-CAS GPS Track

**Effective Date:** November 21, 2024

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information.

## Functionality

The extension:
- Adds a download button to SAC-CAS route portal pages
- Fetches route data from SAC-CAS servers when you click the button
- Generates and downloads a GPX file to your device

All data processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

The extension only communicates with SAC-CAS website (www.sac-cas.ch) to retrieve route information when you explicitly request a download.
